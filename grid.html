Dr. Stephen Strange, known to the world as Doctor Strange, is a prominent figure in the Marvel Universe, celebrated for his extraordinary abilities as the Sorcerer Supreme. Once a renowned neurosurgeon, his life took a dramatic turn following a catastrophic car accident that left him unable to continue his medical career. Driven by desperation and a quest for healing, Strange is journey led him to the mystical realms, where he would ultimately become one of the most powerful and influential sorcerers.
